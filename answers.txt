Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Good for tracking progress, collaborating on group projects, reverting changes.
Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Finance, such as when to sell/buy stocks.
Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging creates a new commit and preserves history of both branches. Rebase rewrites the history of the sources branch.
